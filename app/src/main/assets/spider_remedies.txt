*1. Clean the Area 

Wash the affected area with mild soap and water to prevent infection.

*2. Apply a Cold Compress 

Place an ice pack or a cold compress on the spider sting to reduce pain and swelling.

*3. Elevate the area

If the bite is on an extremity, elevate it to help reduce swelling.

*4. Use Over-The-Counter Creams or Ointments: 

Apply over-the-counter creams or ointments containing hydrocortisone or calamine to alleviate itching and inflammation.

*5. Take an over-the-counter pain reliever: 

If needed, take an over-the-counter pain reliever such as acetaminophen or ibuprofen to manage pain and discomfort.

*If the pain or irritation does not alleviate, visit a doctor immediately.
