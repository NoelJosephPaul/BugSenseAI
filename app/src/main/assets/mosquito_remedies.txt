*1. Ice Pack 

Apply a cold compress or an ice pack to the affected area for about 10 minutes to reduce itching and inflammation.

*2. Aloe Vera

Apply a small amount of pure aloe vera gel to the mosquito bite. Its soothing properties can help alleviate itching and promote healing.

*3. Honey 

Dab a small amount of honey on the mosquito bite. Its antibacterial properties can help prevent infection and reduce itching.

*4. Baking Soda Paste 

Mix a small amount of baking soda with water to create a paste, then apply it to the mosquito bite. This can help relieve itching and reduce inflammation.

*5. Tea Tree Oil

Dilute tea tree oil with a carrier oil (such as coconut oil) and apply it to the mosquito bite. Tea tree oil has anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that can provide relief.
